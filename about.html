About William Go

I recently graduated with a B.S. in Computer Science, summa cum laude, and am currently certified for Amazon's AWS Cloud Practitioner. As an active member of the Kellogg Honors College, consistently earning recognition on both the Dean's and President's Lists throughout my academic career. My coursework included advanced studies in Computer Networks, Software Engineering, Machine Learning, Mobile App Development, and Big Data Analytics, providing me with a well-rounded foundation in both software development and data science.

Throughout my academic journey, I led several impactful projects, such as Pennywise, an expense tracker web application, where I served as Project Manager and Business Analyst. I successfully guided a team in developing the application using React Native and Amazon AWS, integrating services like Plaid for seamless bank data retrieval and budget notifications. I also contributed to the development of a Vision Assistance Application that employed machine learning to support visually impaired individuals, which I presented at the RSCA conference at Cal Poly Pomona. Additionally, I conceptualized and launched WoolyYarnGo!, a mobile app for crochet enthusiasts to track rows and stitches, previously available on the Google Play Store. I am proficient in a variety of programming languages and technologies, including Java, C, Python, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React Native, and Dart.

I am based in Los Angeles County and currently open to work. I am interested in Software Development and Information Technology. I enjoy working on projects that can make a positive change and have interest in making things disability friendly and approachable. I am eager to leverage my technical expertise in software development and mobile applications to contribute to innovative and challenging projects.
